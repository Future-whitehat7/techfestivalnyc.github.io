What is the Tech Festival?

An unforgettable 4 days of technology, learning, and community building.

Why?

We want to provide a platform for students where they can apply their knowledge, learn new skills, and meet others aspiring technologists.

How?

The Tech Festival will feature student and professional panels, a technology conference, a 24-hour hackathon, an countless numbers of ways for students to learn about and prepare for careers in technology.
