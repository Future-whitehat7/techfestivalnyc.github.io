FAQ!

What is a Hackathon?

A Hackathon is a time where people from different backgrounds come together and solve a problem with Technology.

Who can attend?

Anyone can attend. Please have a state ID or some kind of ID with your photograph on it, with you on the day of the event.

How can I register?

Hit the RSVP button on the top, or go to: bit.ly/hackattackRegistration

Do I need a team to participate?

Absolutely not! There will be time for you to find your dream team.

Are there any travel reimbursements?

We are a new team of organizers hosting our first 24-hour Hackathon. Unfortunately at this time, we are not providing any travel reimbursements for the hackathon. Please check back later to see if there are any advancements.

What if I don’t have any coding experience?

Not a problem! We want to help you get started, and we’ll have beginner-focused workshops all day just for that. Even if you don’t have any coding background, you’ll learn and create a project by the end of the night! All you need to bring is a laptop.

What do I need to bring?

You need to bring your state ID, college ID, a laptop + charger, and anything you may need to for sleeping. We will be providing food all day Saturday and Sunday. Note: Kosher food will be served only Saturday night, and Sunday morning. If you do eat Kosher food, you will be responsible for arranging your own meals for Saturday Breakfast and Lunch.

Will there be room for sleeping?

Absolutely yes!!!!! However, please bring your own sleeping bags and pillows.
